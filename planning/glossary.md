# Glossary

**Agency OS** — The full product platform being built.

**Prospect** — A business that may become a client.

**Demo Website** — A preview site created to pitch a prospect.

**Client** — A won prospect with an active relationship.

**Workspace** — A full-page entity view with tabs, header, sidebar, and activity.

**MRR** — Monthly recurring revenue.

**RLS** — Supabase Row Level Security.

**Owner/Admin/Member** — Team roles controlling permissions.
