# Product Principles

## 1. Demo-first sales

The product should support the agency model of building personalized demo sites before outreach.

## 2. Action over information

Dashboards should answer: **What should I do next?**

## 3. Reusable architecture

Major entities should use shared workspace patterns rather than one-off pages.

## 4. Automation-first

Repeated manual work should eventually become a workflow or one-click action.

## 5. Secure by default

Team isolation, role permissions, and Supabase RLS should be treated as core product requirements.

## 6. Build for the agency owner first

Optimize for a solo owner running the business before expanding to larger team workflows.

## 7. Keep the UI calm

Avoid crowded cards, excessive icon buttons, and modal overload. Prefer workspaces, drawers, tabs, and clear page structure.
